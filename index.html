The Event

Where?

General Motors Centre
99 Athol St E.
Oshawa, ON

When?

Sunday November 22, 2015
at 6:05 pm

What?

A fundraiser for the Humane Society of Durham Region. Guests will watch the Oshawa Generals take on the Ottawa 67's and have a chance to win some great prizes.

  Everyone is encouraged to come out the the GM Centre to fill a truck parked at Gate 1 with items from the HSDR Holidy Wish List. Items include: dry dog food, dry cat food, and kitty litter. Humane Society representatives will also be on-site collecting monetary donations that go directly to the shelter.To those unable to make the game you can still help an animal in need by donating online at www.hsdr.org/donations or by texting HSDR to 20222 on your mobile device. By purchasing a tennis ball for $2 general admission ticket holders can participate in our "Go Fetch" game for a chance at some amazing prizes.

The HSDR is made up of a Volunteer Board of Directors, staff members and volunteers. They are a community resource, dedicated to protecting and providing temporary shelter and comfort to all animals in need, while promoting adoptions and re-homing for unwanted, abused, neglected and abandoned animals. The HSDR promotes humane treatment of all living things and works within the law to respond to and investigate all complaints of cruelty and neglect.

www.HSDR.org

The Oshawa Generals are a junior ice hockey team in the Ontario Hockey League. They are based in Oshawa, Ontario. The team is named for General Motors, an early sponsor which has its Canadian headquarters in Oshawa. The Generals are one of the most successful franchises in Canadian Hockey League history.
