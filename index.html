The Event

Where?

General Motors Centre
99 Athol St E.
Oshawa, ON

When?

Sunday November 22, 2015
at 6:05 pm

Why?

A fundraiser for the Humane Society of Durham Region. Guests will watch the Oshawa Generals take on the Ottawa 67's and have a chance to win some great prizes.

What?

Fill a truck parked at Gate 1 with dry dog food, dry cat food, and kitty litter. These items will be driven off the lot at the end of the night directly to the HSDR.

  Humane Society representatives will also be on-site collecting monetary donations that go directly to the shelter. To those unable to make the game you can still help an animal in need by donating online at www.hsdr.org/donations or by texting HSDR to 20222 on your mobile device. Sponsors

The HSDR is made up of a Volunteer Board of Directors, staff members and volunteers. They are a community resource, dedicated to protecting and providing temporary shelter and comfort to all animals in need, while promoting adoptions and re-homing for unwanted, abused, neglected and abandoned animals. The HSDR serves all of Durham Region and is a non-profit organization and no-kill shelter and does not receive any formal government assistance. We rely solely on the contributions of others in order to provide care and a home for the animals.

www.HSDR.org

The Oshawa Generals are a junior ice hockey team in the Ontario Hockey League. They are based in Oshawa, Ontario. The team is named for General Motors, an early sponsor which has its Canadian headquarters in Oshawa. The Generals are one of the most successful franchises in Canadian Hockey League history.

Co-Producer

    We are four Corporate Communications and Public Relations Grad Students from Centennial College. We have the honour of hosting an event for the charity of our choosing, which is the Humane Society of Durham Region. Through our event we hope to spark a conversation and educate the public on the importance of pet rescue and adoption. By raising funds, awareness, and goods for The Humane Society, our ultimate goal is to find these amazing animals their forever homes by this holiday season.

The four of us wanted to take this moment to say that we are extremely proud of the turnout of this event, and the community for coming together for this amazing shelter. We are also proud to announce that were able to present the Humane Society of Durham Region with $3015 in monetary donations, as well as two truck loads of food, toys, treats, and other “wish list” essentials. As an added bonus, the Monday following our event, 18 animals from the HSDR found their forever homes. We would like to encourage everyone to continue to donate and consider adoption, as the HSDR constantly receives new animals who deserve to find their families. Thank you to our sponsors, our guests, The Oshawa Generals and the GM Centre for hosting us, and the Humane Society of Durham Region for allowing us to help out in any way we could.
